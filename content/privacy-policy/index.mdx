## Preferences (cookies / local storage)

To remember your choices, we may store consent and language preferences in browser storage (cookies and/or local storage) using keys such as:

- `consent_status` (consent state)
- `langPref` (language preference)

You can reset these preferences from the consent panel (or by clearing your browser storage).

## Analytics (Google Analytics 4)

Vidforges uses Google Analytics 4 to understand aggregated usage (pages visited, approximate geography, device category, basic events). Analytics is **disabled by default** and is enabled only after explicit consent via the cookie banner.

## External media (YouTube)

Embedded videos use `youtube-nocookie.com`. We show a placeholder until you allow external media. Once enabled, YouTube may set cookies and process data under its own policies.

## Affiliate links

Some outbound links may be affiliate links. When you click an affiliate link, the merchant or affiliate platform may use cookies or tracking parameters to attribute a commission.
