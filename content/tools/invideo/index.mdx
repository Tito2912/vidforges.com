## What InVideo is

InVideo is built around **templates** and fast editing workflows. It’s typically used to:

- produce short ads (UGC-style promos)
- create recurring social series (tips, lists, “top 3”, product highlights)
- repurpose content into multiple formats

It’s not trying to be a cinema-grade editor—it optimizes for speed and consistency.

## Best for

- Performance marketing teams producing variations (hooks, CTAs, formats)
- Solo creators who need volume and a repeatable template workflow
- Ecommerce promo videos and simple explainers

## Plans & credits (checked April 2026)

InVideo’s product and plan details can change, but the Help Center explains how **credits** work for invideo AI plans.

As of April 2026, invideo’s Help Center describes plans like **Plus**, **Max**, **Generative** (and Enterprise), with monthly credit allowances such as:

- **Plus**: 100 credits/mo (good for light usage; credits move fast on higher-quality generations)
- **Max**: 400 credits/mo
- **Generative**: 1,000 credits/mo (where Pro/Ultra generations start to make more sense for volume)

The same Help Center article also provides example credit costs per 1‑minute video (illustrative):

- **Basic**: 2 credits/minute
- **Pro**: 80 credits/minute
- **Ultra**: 160 credits/minute

If you rely heavily on AI generation, plan your workflow around **credits per finished minute** (draft in Basic, reserve Pro/Ultra for key scenes).

## Quick start workflow (so it doesn’t look generic)

1. Pick one template per channel (9:16 for Shorts/Reels, 1:1 for feeds, etc.).
2. Build a brand kit (fonts, colors, logo, lower thirds).
3. Create 3 hooks and 2 CTAs, then generate variations.
4. Replace stock clips with your own footage when possible.
5. Export, test, and keep only what performs—then iterate.
