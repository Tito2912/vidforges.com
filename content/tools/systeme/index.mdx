## What Système.io is

Système.io is an all‑in‑one marketing stack for creators and small businesses. Typical building blocks include:

- landing pages and funnels
- email marketing (campaigns + automations)
- checkout/payments (depending on setup)
- digital products and courses (depending on plan)

It’s especially useful when you want fewer tools and faster execution.

## Best for

- Launching a simple offer (lead magnet → email → checkout)
- Selling a course or digital product without a complex stack
- Running a newsletter + basic automations in one place

## Pricing snapshot (checked April 2026)

Système.io positions itself as an all-in-one stack with a free plan and paid tiers. As of April 2026, the official pricing page lists (monthly):

## Quick start (a real workflow)

1. Pick one offer and one audience. Keep the promise specific.
2. Create a landing page with one CTA (email capture or checkout).
3. Write a 3‑email onboarding sequence (value → proof → offer).
4. Add a basic automation: “if subscribed → send sequence”.
5. Track one metric weekly (conversion rate) and improve copy/offer.
