## What HeyGen is

HeyGen helps you turn a script into a “talking avatar” video. You choose an avatar (or use your own), pick a voice, adjust timing, then export a ready-to-publish clip.

## Best for

- Short explainers for landing pages and product demos
- Social ads and UGC-style creatives
- Onboarding / internal training videos
- Creating multiple language versions of the same video

## Key features (what to look for)

- **Avatars & presenters**: pick a style that matches your brand (realistic vs stylized)
- **Voice & languages**: voice options, pronunciation controls, subtitles
- **Templates & branding**: consistent layouts, colors, and lower thirds
- **Export workflow**: fast iterations, versioning, team review

## Pricing snapshot (checked April 2026)

HeyGen’s pricing changes over time and can vary by region. As of April 2026, the official pricing page lists (monthly):

- **Free**: $0/mo (3 videos/month, up to 1‑min, 720p export)
- **Creator**: $29/mo (up to 30‑min videos, 1080p export, brand kit, watermark removal)
- **Pro**: $99/mo (4K export, faster processing, translation script proofreading, higher Premium usage)
- **Business**: $149/mo (additional seats listed at $20/seat)
- **Enterprise**: custom pricing

Prices exclude taxes/fees. Always confirm current plan limits before subscribing.

## Quick start workflow

1. Start with a short script (60–120 seconds).
2. Choose an avatar and voice (prioritize clarity over “hype”).
3. Add captions (most platforms reward clarity).
4. Export and test 2–3 hooks (first 3 seconds).
5. Track what works, then iterate.
