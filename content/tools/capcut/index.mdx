## What CapCut is

CapCut is a fast, template-friendly video editor. It’s often used to turn raw footage into polished social clips, with captions and quick formatting.

## Best for

- Editing Shorts/Reels/TikTok with consistent style
- Caption-first videos (talking head, UGC, tutorials)
- Quick cuts, b‑roll, and hooks testing
- Turning long footage into short clips

## Key features (what to look for)

- **Captions**: accuracy, styling, and readability
- **Templates**: speed for repeatable formats
- **Effects**: subtle motion and transitions that don’t distract
- **Export**: correct aspect ratios and platform presets

## Pricing snapshot (checked April 2026)

CapCut offers a free experience and paid subscriptions (often **monthly** and **yearly**) for Pro features.

CapCut’s Help Center (Apr 3, 2026) states that **CapCut Pro pricing varies by region, device, and promotions**. For the most accurate price:

1. Log in to CapCut Online / Desktop / Mobile.
2. Click **Upgrade** (or **Join CapCut Pro**) to view the current subscription options in your country.

## Quick start workflow

1. Choose the target format (9:16 vs 16:9).
2. Cut aggressively (remove pauses).
3. Add captions and highlight keywords.
4. Add a simple CTA (comment, follow, click).
5. Export, publish, analyze retention.
