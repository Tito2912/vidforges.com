## What Pictory is

Pictory is built for repurposing. You bring the input (script, article, or a long recording), then you generate a video draft that you can refine with captions, visuals, and pacing.

## Best for

- Turning blog posts into social videos
- Repurposing long videos into Shorts/Reels clips
- Faceless channels that rely on stock visuals + captions
- Marketing videos that need speed over complex editing

## Key features (what to look for)

- **Script → scenes**: how well it structures your content
- **Captions & readability**: typography, spacing, and pacing
- **Branding**: colors, fonts, logos, and reusable templates
- **Export options**: formats that fit your channels

## Quick start workflow

1. Paste a script or import an article.
2. Keep scenes short (1 idea per scene).
3. Fix captions first (clarity beats fancy visuals).
4. Swap visuals where needed (relevance matters).
5. Export, test, iterate.
