Scope

This policy applies to the website vidforges.com and its French section at /fr/. It explains how we use analytics, cookies and similar technologies, and your rights under GDPR.

Data We Collect

  • Essential data (strictly necessary): page loads and resources needed to deliver the site, security logs, consent choices.

Legal Bases

  • Analytics: your consent (opt-in) via Consent Mode v2. Default state is analytics off.

Consent Mode v2

We implement Google Analytics 4 with Consent Mode v2. By default, we set analytics_storage='denied'. When you click “Accept”, we update consent and may (re)fire GA4 configuration. You can reopen settings anytime via the site controls.

  • Preferences are stored in a cookie and localStorage.
  • Declining keeps analytics disabled.
  • No dark patterns; consent is revocable at any time.
