What you’ll find on the Vidforges blog

The Vidforges blog is where we document real workflows built on top of Kling AI. Instead of generic “prompt lists”, we focus on step‑by‑step systems that you can reuse for client work, in‑house marketing, or personal creative projects. Every article is written with non‑technical creators in mind: media buyers, e‑commerce founders, and video editors who want faster output without sacrificing quality or compliance.

You will find frameworks for structuring scripts, examples of vertical video storyboards, and detailed breakdowns of how to turn a simple product idea into a complete 20–30 second video. We also highlight common mistakes when using AI video tools — such as over‑promising results, ignoring accessibility, or forgetting brand constraints — and provide checklists you can apply before publishing.

Our goal is to help you test more creatives with less stress. When new Kling AI features or best practices appear, we update our guides so you always have an up‑to‑date reference. Save the blog, come back to it as you build campaigns, and use the search bar above to find tutorials, optimization tips, and responsible‑use reminders adapted to your everyday production reality.
